ApeX Protocol

ApeX Protocol is a leading permissionless and non-custodial decentralized exchange (DEX), established in 2022.

Its inaugural product, ApeX Pro, facilitates decentralized derivatives trading through an orderbook model, providing USDC and USDT cross-margined perpetual contracts across 45+ pairs with up to 50x leverage.

ApeX's newest product, ApeX Omni, is a modular, intent-centric and chain-agnostic DEX, set to redefine the decentralized trading experience with an aggregated multichain liquidity trading framework, and enhance the DEX trading experience with its innovative features and robust performance.

With an ambitious roadmap and a robust product line, the protocol is positioned to transform the decentralized trading experience for crypto professionals, enthusiasts, and newcomers alike, while steadfastly delivering on the promises of speed, efficiency, and security.
